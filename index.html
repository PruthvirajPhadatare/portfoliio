About Me & Expertise

I am a results-driven **AWS Cloud and DevOps Engineer** with 2 years of professional experience, specializing in leveraging automation to accelerate development and deployment cycles. My core focus is on building robust, scalable, and secure cloud environments.

I thrive on implementing **Infrastructure as Code (IaC)** using **Terraform** to provision and manage critical AWS infrastructure components, ensuring environment consistency and rapid, repeatable deployment across multiple stages. This approach has demonstrably reduced manual configuration efforts and infrastructure build time.

My expertise extends to building and maintaining zero-touch **CI/CD pipelines** using tools like **Jenkins** and **ArgoCD**, streamlining microservice deployment to environments like **AWS EKS**. I am proficient in the full DevOps lifecycle, from containerization (**Docker, Kubernetes**) and configuration management (**Helm**) to establishing comprehensive monitoring systems (**Prometheus, Grafana**) for high availability and performance.
